Notable Shifts · Account Trends

Four patterns behind the YoY growth.

1 · Rocket ecosystem deepening

~$314K of top-15 revenue — up from $174K in Q1 '25

  • Rocket Central: $185K (+20% YoY) — still the #1 account by a wide margin.
  • Rocket Bedrock Extended Stay Guest (NEW): 356 nights at $162 ADR — a new pattern of longer-stay corporate housing distinct from transient Rocket bookings.
  • Rocket Mortgage (NEW): 203 nights direct-sourced from the Rocket Mortgage entity.
  • Rock Events: $32K (+62%) — group/events demand from the same ownership ecosystem.

2 · Step-change growth in travel-managed accounts

Off small 2025 bases — now meaningful contributors

  • Henry Ford Health: $2.7K → $35.7K (+1,226%) on 163 nights.
  • AMEXGBT: $2.4K → $31.4K (+1,217%) at $317 ADR — strongest rate in the top 15.
  • Detroit Opera House: $11.7K → $33.1K (+184%) on 242 nights — a high-volume, lower-rate pattern (cast/crew housing).

3 · Entertainment & corporate-housing newcomers

Six net-new top-15 accounts

  • Shady Records: $22.1K at $346 ADR — highest rate in top 15.
  • The Line Studios: $17.3K (62 nights) — creative/production bookings.
  • Alacrity Solutions: $22.4K — insurance/claims extended stay.
  • Additional new-to-2026 relationships outside top 15: Churchill Living, Sirva, Furnished Quarters, Dwellworks, Ally Financial, Ford Motor Co.

4 · Pistons, legacy sports, and relocations softening

Where revenue moved away

  • Detroit Pistons: $70.6K → $30.2K (-57%) — the only top-15 decliner.
  • Outside top 15, lost in 2026: "Rock" ($45K → $0), ALE Solutions ($21K → $0), Navan Lodging ($14K → $0), SilverDoor ($13K → $0), Nomad ($11K → $0), Detroit Tigers ($8K → $0), Detroit Lions ($6K → $0).
  • Bedrock LLC direct: $27.8K → $4.0K (-86%) — volume appears to have shifted into the new "Rocket Bedrock Extended Stay" channel.
Section 3 · Takeaways

The account mix is healthier — and more concentrated — than last year.

Strengths

Top 15 revenue up +70% YoY; six net-new logos in the top 15; Rocket ecosystem is broadening across transient, extended-stay, and events. Healthcare and TMC wins (Henry Ford Health, AMEXGBT) expand the mix beyond the Rocket anchor.

Watchout

Rocket + Bedrock + Rock Events account for ~51% of top-15 revenue. Anchor concentration is a feature of the site, but worth monitoring as the book grows.

Opportunity

Several 2025 accounts disappeared in 2026 (ALE Solutions, Navan, SilverDoor, Nomad, Detroit Tigers/Lions). Some of these are reallocations; others are winnable with re-engagement. Worth a lapsed-account audit in Q2.

Next step

Understand the Detroit Pistons -57% drop (rate, availability, or relationship shift?) and formalize the Rocket Bedrock Extended Stay channel — it's producing but hasn't had a full quarter yet.
